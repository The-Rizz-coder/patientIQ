💡 Key Clinical Finding
Patients with 3+ prior inpatient visits show a readmission rate of 23.2% — nearly 3× higher than low-utilization patients. Prior hospital utilization is the strongest predictor of 30-day readmission.
📋 Intervention Recommendation
High Risk patients (7,958) should receive intensive post-discharge follow-up within 48 hours. Targeting this segment alone addresses ~18% of all readmissions in the population.
